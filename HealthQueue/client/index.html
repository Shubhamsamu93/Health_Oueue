About HealthQueue

HealthQueue is a smart hospital appointment management system designed to reduce overcrowding and long waiting times in hospitals. It helps patients book appointments easily while allowing doctors to manage their schedules efficiently.

For Patients

Book appointments quickly, choose time slots, and get estimated waiting time.

For Doctors

Manage patient load, approve/reject bookings, and control availability.

Smart System

Predict waiting time based on bookings and reduce hospital crowd efficiently.
